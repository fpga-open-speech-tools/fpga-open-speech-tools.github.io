Audio Blade

The Audio Blade is an audio signal processing platform that includes an iWave Arria 10 System on Module. This high performance platform is perfect for researchers who wish to develop advanced audio processing algorithms.

Details
Audio Research

Audio Research is an audio processing daughter card designed for the Reflex Achilles Arria 10 SoM. The daughter card connects standard audio interfaces to the Arria 10 through the AD1939 codec. It also provides an RJ45 connection for the Audio Array.

Details
Audio Mini

The Audio Mini is an audio processing cape designed for the Altera DE-10 Nano. This cape contains an AD1939 audio codec that is used to sample audio from the line input and play back audio to the headphone output.

Details
Microphone Array

The Microphone Array is a 16 Element MEMS Microphone Array used for collecting about data for beam forming applications. It is designed to connect to the Audio Blade or Audio Research using a standard Ethernet cable.
