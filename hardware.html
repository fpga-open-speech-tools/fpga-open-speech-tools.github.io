Audio Mini

The Audio Mini is an audio processing cape designed for the Altera DE-10 Nano. This cape contains an AD1939 audio codec that is used to sample audio from the line input and play back audio to the headphone output.

Audio Blade

The Audio Blade is an audio signal processing platform that includes an iWave Arria 10 System on Module. This high performance platform is perfect for researchers who wish to develop advanced audio processing algorithms.
